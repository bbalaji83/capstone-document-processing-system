Company Travel Policy 2024

Domestic Travel:
All domestic travel must be booked at least 7 days in advance.
Economy class is standard for flights under 3 hours.
Hotel bookings must not exceed $150 per night.
Meal allowance is $50 per day for domestic travel.

International Travel:
International travel requires manager approval 2 weeks in advance.
Business class is permitted for flights exceeding 8 hours.
Hotel bookings must not exceed $250 per night.
Meal allowance is $75 per day for international travel.

Reimbursement:
All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of travel.
Original receipts are required for expenses over $25.
Reimbursements are processed within 2 weeks of submission.
